# Practice Note Template

## Title

Provide a short title for the observation or lesson.

## Context

Describe the project context, task type, or workflow situation where this note was observed.

## Observation

State what happened as concretely as possible.

## Why It Matters

Explain why the observation affects repeatability, consistency, quality, or speed.

## Proposed Guidance

- Describe the candidate rule, pattern, or adjustment.
- State whether this looks reusable or context-specific.

## Evidence

- List examples, artifacts, or outcomes that support the note.
- Mention uncertainty when the evidence is limited.

## Status

Choose one and explain why:

- candidate
- adopted
- rejected
- needs more evidence

## Next Step

Describe what should happen next: test again, promote to a standard, turn into a workflow, or discard.
